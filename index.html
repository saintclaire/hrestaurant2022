Since 1995, Homefoods has had the privilege of fusing flavors from all over the world and presenting them tastefully on a table for homes and kitchens.

The singular passion to gather authentic spices and seasonings from all over the world has made Homefoods authentic purveyors of quality, innovative, ethnic, culinary and gourmet range of food products.

From presenting authentic spices and seasonings to the world, Homefoods, through its subsidiary restaurants, now presents authentic Ghanaian food and recipes to the world.

The H Restaurant

Grandma to the world!

Homefoods was founded as an agro business in 1995 on a  kitchen table, in a rented home, with one (1) staff and my daughter.

The singular passion to gather authentic spices and food products from all over the world, has made Homefoods purveyors of  quality, innovative, ethnic, culinary and gourmet array of food products.

Cooking was done amidst love, laughter and joy in the kitchen where - my grandmother's and mother's passion for cooking took precedence. Over simmering pots of stews and soups, family recipes have been passed on from generation on to generation.

I hereby welcome you to ' The H Restaurant ', where indigenous food is cooked with love, presenting Ghanaian cuisines and West Africa to the World.

Felicia Twumasi
Founder and CEO
